Privacy Policy for Penny

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Penny ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile photo (optional)
  • Financial Data: Income, expenses, categories, descriptions, and dates you manually enter
  • Savings Plans: Goals, target amounts, percentages, and descriptions
  • Payment Information: Subscription status (managed by Apple App Store; we do not store payment card details)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, crash logs
  • Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics to improve app performance

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Penny app functionality
  • Generate AI-powered insights and personality reports
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Process subscription payments (via Apple)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important updates about the service
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase/Firestore: Cloud-based encrypted database (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Control: Strict user authentication ensures you can only access your own data
  • Data Isolation: Each user's data is completely isolated from other users

5. Third-Party Services

Penny integrates with the following third-party services:

5.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, database storage, cloud functions
  • Data shared: Account information, expense data
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 OpenAI

  • Purpose: Generate AI personality reports and financial insights
  • Data shared: Aggregated spending patterns (no personally identifiable information)
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

5.3 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription management and in-app purchases
  • Data shared: User ID, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5.4 Google Sheets (Optional)

  • Purpose: Sync expenses with your personal Google Sheet (if you enable this feature)
  • Data shared: Expense records you choose to sync
  • You control: Whether to enable this feature

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the app (Firebase, OpenAI)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Download your data via Google Sheets sync
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Opt-out: Disable optional features like Google Sheets sync
  • Correct: Edit or correct your information at any time

8. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup copies deleted within 90 days

9. Children's Privacy

Penny is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your residence, including the United States (Google Cloud Platform servers). We ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, use the in-app Delete Account feature or contact us.

12. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For European users, we comply with GDPR:

  • Legal Basis: Consent and contract performance
  • Data Controller: Penny App
  • Your Rights: Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact via email below

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Penny after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@pennyapp.com
  • In-App: Settings > Support

15. Agreement

By using Penny, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.
