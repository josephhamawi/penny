Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure Firebase infrastructure. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never share your financial information with third parties.

How does the Google Sheets sync work?

You can optionally sync your expense data to your personal Google Sheet. You maintain full ownership and control, and can revoke access anytime through your Google Account settings.
